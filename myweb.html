Biography

José A. Padrón Hidalgo received the B.Sc degree in Telecommunications and Electronics from the University of Pinar del Río, Cuba, in 2015. He is currently working toward the Ph.D degree in Electronics.

His current research interest includes developing algorithms in order to detect anomalous and extreme changes for Remote Sensing imagery with the Image and Signal Processing Group with the Universitat de València.
